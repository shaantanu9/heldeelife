# Project Brief: heldeelife

## Project Overview

**heldeelife** is a modern e-commerce platform specializing in Ayurveda and modern medicine products. The platform combines traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with contemporary healthcare solutions, providing users with a holistic approach to health and wellness.

## Core Mission

To make authentic Ayurvedic and modern medicine products accessible to everyone through a seamless, user-friendly digital platform that emphasizes education, quality, and customer care.

## Project Goals

1. **E-commerce Platform**: Enable customers to browse, search, and purchase Ayurvedic and modern medicine products
2. **Content Hub**: Provide educational blog content about Ayurveda, health, and wellness
3. **User Experience**: Deliver a clean, minimal, and intuitive interface that reflects the holistic nature of Ayurveda
4. **Admin Management**: Empower administrators to manage products, content, and orders efficiently
5. **Scalability**: Build a foundation that can grow with the business

## Target Audience

- Health-conscious individuals seeking Ayurvedic solutions
- Customers looking for natural and traditional medicine alternatives
- People interested in holistic wellness approaches
- Medical professionals and practitioners

## Key Requirements

### Must-Have Features (MVP)

1. **User Authentication & Authorization**
   - Email and phone number login
   - User profiles
   - Role-based access control (User/Admin)

2. **Product Management**
   - Product catalog with categories
   - Product search and filtering
   - Product detail pages
   - Shopping cart functionality
   - Checkout process

3. **Order Management**
   - Order creation and tracking
   - Order history for users
   - Order management for admins

4. **Content Management**
   - Blog system with SEO optimization
   - Category and tag management
   - Rich text editor for content creation

5. **Admin Dashboard**
   - Product CRUD operations
   - Blog post management
   - Order management
   - User management (future)

6. **User Experience**
   - Responsive design (mobile-first)
   - Fast page loads
   - Intuitive navigation
   - Shopping cart persistence

### Future Enhancements (Post-MVP)

1. **Payment Integration**
   - Multiple payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, etc.)
   - Payment status tracking
   - Refund management

2. **Advanced Features**
   - Product reviews and ratings
   - Wishlist functionality
   - Product recommendations
   - Email notifications
   - Order tracking with shipping integration
   - Inventory management UI
   - Analytics dashboard
   - Email verification
   - Password reset functionality
   - Social login (Google, Facebook)

3. **Content Features**
   - Doctor profiles and booking
   - Health insights/articles
   - Newsletter subscription
   - Product recommendations based on health conditions

## Success Criteria

- Users can browse and purchase products seamlessly
- Admins can manage content and products efficiently
- Platform is SEO-optimized for discoverability
- Fast, responsive user experience
- Secure authentication and data handling
- Scalable architecture for future growth

## Constraints

- Node.js 20+ required
- TypeScript for type safety
- Supabase for database and authentication
- Next.js 14 App Router architecture
- shadcn/ui for UI components

## Timeline

- **Phase 1 (MVP)**: Core e-commerce functionality
- **Phase 2**: Payment integration and advanced features
- **Phase 3**: Analytics, recommendations, and optimization
