## Key Research Findings

### High Impact Strategies

1. **Abandoned Cart Recovery**
   - **Impact**: 10-30% recovery rate with automated emails
   - **Implementation**: Automated email sequences, exit-intent popups
   - **Best Practice**: Send first email within 30 minutes

2. **Checkout Optimization**
   - **Impact**: 35% reduction in abandonment with guest checkout
   - **Implementation**: Guest checkout option, progress indicators, minimal fields
   - **Best Practice**: Reduce form fields to essential only

3. **Site Speed**
   - **Impact**: 1-second delay = 7% conversion loss
   - **Implementation**: Image optimization, code splitting, CDN usage
   - **Best Practice**: Target <2 second load time

4. **Mobile Optimization**
   - **Impact**: 50%+ of traffic is mobile
   - **Implementation**: Responsive design, touch-friendly targets, mobile-first
   - **Best Practice**: Test on real devices, not just emulators

5. **Social Proof**
   - **Impact**: 15-25% increase in conversions
   - **Implementation**: Reviews, ratings, testimonials, sales counts
   - **Best Practice**: Show recent activity, verified purchases

6. **Personalization**
   - **Impact**: 20-30% increase in revenue
   - **Implementation**: Recommendations, dynamic content, user history
   - **Best Practice**: Use browsing and purchase history

7. **Charm Pricing**
   - **Impact**: 2-5% increase in conversions ($19.99 vs $20)
   - **Implementation**: Use .99 endings, display savings clearly
   - **Best Practice**: Show original price for comparison

8. **Free Shipping**
   - **Impact**: 30% reduction in cart abandonment
   - **Implementation**: Free shipping thresholds, clear messaging
   - **Best Practice**: Show progress to free shipping threshold

9. **Live Chat**
   - **Impact**: 20-30% increase in conversions
   - **Implementation**: Chat widgets, proactive messaging
   - **Best Practice**: Respond within 30 seconds

10. **Product Videos**
    - **Impact**: 6-30% increase in sales per product
    - **Implementation**: Demo videos, testimonials, how-to guides
    - **Best Practice**: Keep videos under 2 minutes
